FAQs

Common Questions

When can I come for a visit?

You can visit Monday to Saturday between 8:00 AM – 1:00 PM. We recommend calling ahead so a teacher is available to meet you.

What documents do I need for admission?

Birth certificate, 2 photos, parent ID, address proof, and vaccination record. See the full list on our Admission page.

Do you provide transport?

Please contact us directly to enquire about transport availability in your area.

What are the fees?

Fee details are shared during your school visit. Please WhatsApp us or call to get the latest fee structure.
